HOW WE DO IT

BRING YOUR IDEAS TO LIFE

Getting your ideas from concept to completion can be an arduous task. we help transform your designs into workable 3D production drawings and connect you with reliable suppliers who meet your criteria for either large-scale, low cost production or low volume, high-end niche production.

MEETING STANDARDS

You and your customers demand quality. This is why we use our local expertise to prepare factories for auditing. we help factories pass audits such as SEDEX, BSCI or even client specific internal audits such as H & M's.

SHIP WITH CERTAINTY

Getting your product from factory to destination is the final step that requires precision and care. we are a team of experts with proven experience in shipping goods worldwide, either directly to stores or as consolidated shipments for warehouse delivery.
